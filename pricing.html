FAQs

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know before you hit the road.

Can I switch plans mid-month?

Yes — upgrades are instant and prorated. Downgrades take effect from the next billing cycle.

What documents do I need to rent?

A valid driving licence (held for at least 1 year), one government-issued photo ID, and a credit/debit card for the security deposit.

Is fuel included in the price?

Fuel is not included – you rent with a full tank and return it full. Electric vehicles get free charging at Stackly partner hubs.

What happens if I exceed the km limit?

Extra kilometres are billed at $0.15/km for Economy, $0.25/km for SUV & Luxury, and $0.35/km for Sports cars. Elite members get unlimited kilometres.

How does the security deposit work?

A hold is placed on your card at pickup ($100–$800 depending on car category). Released within 3–5 business days after a clean return.

Can I cancel or modify a booking?

Starter: 24 hrs before pickup. Pro: 48 hrs. Elite: anytime. Modifications are always free.
