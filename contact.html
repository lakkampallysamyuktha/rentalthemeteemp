Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your rental hours?

We offer 24/7 pickup and drop‑off at most locations. Our support team is available around the clock via phone and WhatsApp.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit/debit cards, digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and corporate billing for fleet accounts.

What documents do I need to rent?

A valid US driver's license (held for at least 1 year), government‑issued ID, and a credit card for the security deposit.

Can I return the car to a different city?

Yes, one‑way rentals are available across the US for an additional fee. Contact our support team to arrange it.
