Our Journey

12 Years of Driving Dreams

Every milestone shaped who we are today.

2012

Humble Beginnings

Three friends, six cars, and one Los Angeles garage. Stackly launched with a simple promise — arrive in style, every time.

Going National

By 2015 we had expanded across 20 US metros, partnered with 5-star hotels, and introduced America's first chauffeur-driven luxury rental tier.

2015
2019

Going Green

Stackly became the first US rental platform to launch an all-electric tier — Tesla, Porsche Taycan and Hyundai IONIQ, zero emissions, maximum thrill.

50,000 Happy Customers

2024 marked a record year. 50k customers, 4.9★ rating on every platform, and a fleet upgrade to 500+ vehicles across economy, SUV, luxury, and sports.
